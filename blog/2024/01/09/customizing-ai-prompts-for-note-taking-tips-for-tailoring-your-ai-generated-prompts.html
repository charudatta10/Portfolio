    Customizing AI Prompts for Note-Taking: Tips for Tailoring Your AI-Generated Prompts¶

    In the age of artificial intelligence (AI), note-taking has evolved beyond scribbling on paper. AI-powered tools now assist us in capturing, organizing, and summarizing information. But how can we make these tools work best for our unique needs? The answer lies in customizing AI prompts—the instructions we give to AI models to generate specific content. In this blog post, we'll explore how to tailor AI prompts for effective note-taking.

    Why Customize AI Prompts?¶

    1. Precision: Generic prompts may yield generic results. Customizing prompts ensures that the AI understands your specific requirements.
    2. Context: Your context matters. Custom prompts provide relevant context, leading to more accurate and useful responses.
    3. Efficiency: Well-crafted prompts save time. Instead of sifting through irrelevant content, you get what you need upfront.

    High-Context AI Prompts for Note-Taking¶

    1. Key Takeaways Prompt¶

    • Purpose: Summarize lengthy notes into essential points.
    • Customization: Use the following template: 
      "My notes are below in triple quotes:
\"\"\"
{selectedText}
\"\"\"
Write a markdown list (using dashes) of key takeaways from my notes. Be detailed and don't leave any information out."
    • Result: AI generates a concise list of key takeaways.

    2. Action Items Prompt¶

    • Purpose: Identify actionable steps from selected text.
    • Customization: 
      "My note is below in triple quotes:
\"\"\"
{selectedText}
\"\"\"
Write a todo list of action items from my notes using the following format:
<first action item>
<second action item>
<third action item>
<fourth action item>"
    • Result: AI generates a task list based on actionable content.

    3. Copy Editor Prompt¶

    • Purpose: Improve readability, grammar, and punctuation.
    • Customization: 
      "Act as a copy editor. Go through the text in triple quotes below. Edit it for spelling mistakes, grammar issues, punctuation, and flow. Format the text into appropriately sized paragraphs.
\"\"\"
{selectedText}
\"\"\"
Return only the edited text."
    • Result: AI refines your content, making it error-free and reader-friendly.

    4. Structured Voice Notes Prompt¶

    • Purpose: Format transcribed voice notes into readable paragraphs.
    • Customization: 
      "Act as a copy editor. Go through the text in triple quotes below. Edit it for spelling mistakes, grammar issues, punctuation, and flow. Format the text into appropriately sized paragraphs.
\"\"\"
{selectedText}
\"\"\"
Return only the edited text."
    • Result: AI organizes the text into well-structured paragraphs.

    Tips for Effective Customization¶

    1. Be Specific: Clearly define what you want the AI to do.
    2. Experiment: Test different prompts to find what works best.
    3. Review and Refine: Always review AI-generated content and make necessary adjustments.

    Conclusion¶

    Customizing AI prompts empowers you to extract maximum value from AI tools. Whether you're summarizing, editing, or structuring notes, tailor your prompts to fit your unique needs. Remember, AI is a powerful assistant, but your customization makes it truly effective.
